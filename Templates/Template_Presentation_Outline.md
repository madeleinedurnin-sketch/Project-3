# Presentation Slides Template (Outline)

## Slide 1: Title Slide
*   Project Title
*   Group Number & Member Names
*   Date

## Slide 2: Social Issue Context & Framing (3 min)
*   **The Problem:** What is the social issue? (e.g., "The marginalization of women's sports in digital media").
*   **Context:** Brief real-world context (e.g., "The Clark-Reese rivalry created a spike in discourse...").
*   **Research Question:** One clear sentence.

## Slide 3: Data & Scope (1 min)
*   **Dataset:** Source, Size, Dates.
*   **Filter/Scope:** "We focused on the top 500 comments on Instagram..."
*   **Why this data?** Brief justification.

## Slide 4: Analytical Strategy (2 min)
*   **Key Concept:** "We measured [Concept] by looking for..."
*   **The Method:** "We performed a sentiment analysis/keyword count..."
*   **Validation:** "We checked for accuracy by..."

## Slide 5: Key Findings (2 min)
*   **Finding 1:** Major pattern observed (with data evidence).
*   **Finding 2:** Major pattern observed (with data evidence).
*   **Visuals:** Insert a chart or a screenshot of representative data here.

## Slide 6: Interpretation & Implications (2 min)
*   **The "So What?":** Connect findings back to social power, legitimacy, or inequality.
*   **Implication:** "For sport managers, this means..."
*   **Ethical Note:** Briefly mention one limitation or ethical boundary.
